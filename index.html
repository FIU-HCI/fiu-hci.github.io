FIU HCI Seminar

The FIU Human-Computer Interaction Seminar brings together faculty, graduate students, and researchers interested in the design and study of interactive technologies. The seminar provides a space to share research ideas, present work in progress, and discuss emerging topics in human-computer interaction.

As the HCI research community at FIU continues to grow, the seminar series will highlight ongoing research, student presentations, and collaborative discussions across labs and disciplines.
